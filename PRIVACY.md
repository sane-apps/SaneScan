# SaneScan Privacy

SaneScan is designed so scans stay on your device unless you explicitly share or export them.

## What The App Does Not Collect

SaneScan does not collect:

- Personal data
- Scan contents
- OCR text
- Photos or documents
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics events
- Accounts or profile information

App Store privacy is configured as **Data Not Collected**.

## Local Data

SaneScan stores scan images, recognized text, and document metadata in the app container on your device. PDF export uses the system share sheet, so exported files go only where you choose to send them.

## Permissions

SaneScan requests permissions only when a feature needs them:

- Camera access is requested when you start a document scan.
- Photos access is requested when you import an existing image.

The app does not require an account, cloud sync, or a SaneApps server for scanning, OCR, cleanup, or PDF export.

## Purchases

SaneScan uses Apple StoreKit for Pro purchases. Apple processes App Store purchases; SaneScan does not receive payment card details.
