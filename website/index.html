For documents you cannot treat casually.

Use SaneScan for contracts, intake forms, signed packets, tax papers, receipts, and family archive photos that should stay under your control.

  • ✓Scan new paper with Apple's system document scanner.
  • ✓Import older photos from the Photos picker.
  • ✓Recognize text locally and export PDFs through the iOS share sheet.

Private by default.

SaneScan does not need an account and does not upload scan contents to SaneApps servers. Camera and Photos access are requested only when you start the matching action.

No account wall

Open the app, scan or import, and keep working. There is no login system between you and your documents.

Local document work

Image cleanup, OCR, and PDF creation are designed around local device workflows and Apple system surfaces.

You choose where PDFs go

PDF exports use the iOS share sheet, so you choose the destination instead of sending documents through a SaneApps account.

SaneScan Pro

SaneScan Pro unlocks unlimited private scans, batch import up to 50 images, local OCR, and PDF export. Purchases are handled by Apple in the app.

  • ✓ Unlimited document and receipt scans
  • ✓ Batch import up to 50 images for archive cleanup
  • ✓ Annual Pro subscription handled by Apple in the app

Basic remains available for light use with 10 scans per month. Pro is the plan for regular document, receipt, and archive work.
