Built for the paper people actually keep.

Receipts, intake forms, signed packets, tax papers, and family archive photos all fit in the same private local library.

  • ✓Scan new paper with Apple's system document scanner.
  • ✓Import older photos from the Photos picker.
  • ✓Clean pages, recognize text, and export PDFs from the device.

Privacy is the product shape.

The SaneScan app does not need an account, does not track users, and does not upload scan contents to SaneApps servers. If you email for updates or support, SaneApps receives the email address and message you choose to send.

No account wall

Open the app, scan or import, and keep working. There is no login system between you and your documents.

Local document work

Image cleanup, OCR, and PDF creation are designed around local device workflows and Apple's system surfaces.

System sharing

Exports use the iOS share sheet, so you choose where a PDF goes instead of sending it through a SaneApps account.

SaneScan Pro

Basic includes 10 scans per month and smaller imports. Pro unlocks unlimited scans and batch import up to 50 images through StoreKit.
